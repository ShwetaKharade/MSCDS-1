In the final of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024, India clinched a thrilling victory over South Africa by seven runs. Held at Kensington Oval in Bridgetown, Barbados, the match saw India set a competitive total of 178/6. Despite a strong chase, South Africa fell short, finishing at 171/9. Key performances included a crucial fifty from Virat Kohli and a pivotal bowling display by Jasprit Bumrah, who took vital wickets and doing economical bowling. This win marked India's  ICC T20 World Cup title in 13 years.
